About

Sarah Johnson is an award-winning tech journalist and AI researcher with over 10 years of experience covering technology trends, artificial intelligence, and innovation. Her work has been featured in major tech publications, and she's known for making complex topics accessible to everyone.

As the host of Tech Chronicles, Sarah brings her expertise and passion for technology to listeners every week, exploring the latest developments in AI, machine learning, and emerging technologies. She has interviewed industry leaders, researchers, and entrepreneurs from around the world.

Sarah holds a Master's degree in Computer Science and has worked as a researcher at leading tech companies before transitioning to journalism. She's passionate about ethical AI development and the responsible use of technology.
